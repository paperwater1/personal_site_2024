Daxbot

Created a GPT4-based "virtual version" of Dax Flame.

Daxbot utilized vector embeddings and a custom retrieval system of all of Dax Flame's YouTube video transcripts and literary work in order to simulate a persistent memory of its own past.

Jakobot

Created a GPT4-based "virtual version" of Jakob Longcob.

Jakobot utilized vector embeddings of a 365 day diary to simulate Jakob's memory of the past year. Special care was taken to ensure that Jakobot responded using a similar style to the real life Jakob.

Valorant Potency Score
